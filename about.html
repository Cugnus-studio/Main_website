Our Story

From challenge to innovation

At just 15 years old, Tanuj Sharma and Sparsh Jain embarked on an extraordinary journey to revolutionize the tech industry. Born from the challenges they faced as young innovators with limited resources, Cygnus emerged as a testament to their determination and ingenuity.

The journey began when they realized that many brilliant young minds were held back not by lack of talent, but by limited access to resources. This revelation sparked their mission to create solutions that would democratize technology and empower the next generation of innovators.

Starting with Nano_Process_On, they transformed the concept of portable computing, making it accessible to everyone. Their innovation continued with MoMose, breaking barriers between mobile and desktop computing, and Overlayerd, pushing the boundaries of spatial computing.

Despite their young age, Tanuj and Sparsh's deep understanding of technology, combined with their firsthand experience of resource constraints, has given them a unique perspective on innovation. They've proven that age is not a barrier to creating meaningful change in the world.
